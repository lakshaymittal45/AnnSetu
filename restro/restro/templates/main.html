    How It Works

    Donors list extra food

    Restaurants, grocery stores, and individuals can easily add surplus food to the platform in just a few clicks.

    Recipients find and request meals

    NGOs, shelters, and those in need can browse available food nearby and request it instantly.

    Pickups are arranged for delivery

    Donors and recipients coordinate pickups, ensuring the food reaches the right hands on time.

    How We Do It?

    In India, nearly 40% of the food produced goes to waste, while over 195 million people struggle with hunger every day. At the same time, restaurants, grocery stores, and households often have excess food that could still be consumed. AnnDaan is working to change this by redirecting surplus food to those who need it the most.

    Our platform allows individuals, restaurants, and businesses to donate extra food easily. NGOs, shelters, and those in need can find and request meals nearby, ensuring no food goes to waste.
